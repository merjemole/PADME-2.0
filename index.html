FAQs

Where do you ship to?

Anywhere in the world! We are able to ship orders globally for a €5 shipping fee.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Our production and shipping times vary depending on your location. Typically, orders are processed within 2–3 business days, and shipping times range from 5–15 business days. For a more specific estimate, check your confirmation email or contact us.

What is your return policy?

Unfortunately, as each t-shirt is made to order for you, we are unable to accept returns unless due to a print error. If your t-shirt has been delivered with a print error, please send a photo alongside your order number to returns@brandteesai.com.
